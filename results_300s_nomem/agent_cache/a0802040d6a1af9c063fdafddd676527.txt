Last came a little feeble squeaking voice (`That's Bill,' thought Alice), `Well, I hardly know. I'm better now--but I'm too much frightened to say. I fell upon the roof, you know,' and the words were accompanied by a general chorus of `E-h, my dear, that is bad!'
`Digging for apples, indeed!' said the Rabbit angrily. `Here! Come and help me out of THIS!' (Sounds of more broken glass.)
